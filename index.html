The Algorithm

JPF MLAT Engine

Our proprietary execution stack is built in Python, utilizing the Futu OpenD API to route orders across 30+ assets. The system is engineered for speed, currently clocking sub-85ms latency in forward-testing.

Hybrid Neural Networks

Moving beyond simple price action, we deploy LSTM and Transformer architectures. These models ingest macroeconomic variables (Yield Curves, VIX) to predict structural market shifts rather than just noise.

Data Normalization

Garbage in, garbage out. Our ETL pipelines scrub and normalize tick-level data, ensuring that every trade signal is based on statistically significant residuals, not dirty data.
