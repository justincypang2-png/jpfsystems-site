About The Firm

Engineering the Future of Finance

J.P. Freglien Systems is a quantitative technology holding company established in Durham, United Kingdom. We operate at the intersection of academic economics and high-frequency infrastructure, rejecting the notion that theoretical models cannot withstand the rigour of live execution.

Founded by Justin P., the firm was built to operationalise the concept of "Theory in Practice." We are not a traditional fund driven by sentiment; we are a laboratory where Linear Regression frameworks and automated risk protocols replace human bias.
